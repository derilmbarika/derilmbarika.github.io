About me

It started with a how-to, in Cameroon.

My love for making videos started young, back home in Cameroon. One of the first things I ever filmed was a simple guide showing people how to connect a gas bottle to a gas plate. It sounds small, but it is the whole reason I do this. I have always been driven to show people how things are done.

If you know me, you know I want the best for the people around me. I care so much that I will get on your case, and yes, sometimes I get irritated, all because I do not want you to repeat the mistakes I have already lived through. My friends were never enough for that instinct, so now I make videos to reach further, to teach and to inspire whoever is watching.

I do this for the love first, before anything else.

The only reason you ever fail is because you gave up.
